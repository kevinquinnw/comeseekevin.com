My Values


MISSION: To develop online solutions for all business clients. I create custom designs to allow owners to manage their own content and where I can propel efficiency. My goal is to make your life a little bit easier when it comes to web development, or any software design.


VISION: I love to teach programming and I love to code in general. Programming is incredibly empowering, it takes a lot of curiosity to become great at it. You ask why things work, and by learning how a system works, you learn how to manipulate the system. You are in control. My curiosity has led me to learn more and why things work - which is essential to manipulate a computer system. I intend to share my vision that programming is a creative act, and anybody can learn if they are curious enough to figure it out.
